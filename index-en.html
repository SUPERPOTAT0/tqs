Who We Are

Trend for Quality Systems (TQS) is a leading consulting and certification body, dedicated to empowering businesses through the implementation of management systems compliant with international standards.

We provide tailored solutions to ensure compliance, improve performance, and enhance confidence in your operations and products.

Our Distinguished Services

We offer a comprehensive suite of certification and training services across a wide range of global ISO standards.

ISO 9001:2015

Quality Management System (QMS) to ensure customer requirements are met and to drive continuous improvement.

ISO 14001:2015

Environmental Management System (EMS) to commit to environmental responsibility and reduce environmental impact.

ISO 45001:2018

Occupational Health & Safety (OH&S) Management System to protect workers and mitigate risks.

ISO 22000:2018

Food Safety Management System (FSMS) to ensure food safety throughout all stages of the food chain.

ISO 13485:2016

Quality Management System for Medical Devices to ensure the quality and safety of medical equipment.

ISO 21001:2018

Educational Organizations Management System (EOMS) to improve the quality of educational services.

ISO 50001:2018

Energy Management System (EnMS) to enhance energy efficiency and reduce operational costs.
